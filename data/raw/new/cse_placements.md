# IIT Mandi CSE — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 89.32% | 26.55 LPA | 22.23 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 98% | 34.46 LPA | 27 LPA |

## Software vs Core Split

- Software/SWE roles: ~70–80% of placed CSE students
- Data Science/ML roles: ~10–15%
- Core CS/Research roles: ~5%
- Finance/Quant: ~2–3%
- Other: remainder

## Key Recruiters (Historical, Subject to Change)

**High-paying product companies (occasional):**
- Microsoft (India R&D, Azure)
- Amazon (SDE roles, AWS)
- Adobe Systems (R&D Noida)
- Walmart Global Tech (SDE)
- Samsung R&D Institute India (Noida/Bangalore)
- Qualcomm (occasionally)

**Mid-tier product/service:**
- Nagarro
- Persistent Systems
- Mphasis
- Hexaware (occasionally)
- Publicis Sapient (occasionally)

**Startups:**
- Various Series A/B funded startups (year-dependent)

**Analytics/Data:**
- Analytics firms (EXL, WNS — occasionally)

## PPO (Pre-Placement Offer) Discussion

- PPOs happen from internships
- Companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe have given PPOs to IIT Mandi CSE interns
- PPO rate: estimated 10–20% of internship holders; small numbers
- Strategy: securing a strong 3rd year internship is the best PPO route

## Internship Ecosystem

- 3rd year summer: primary internship season
- Companies visiting for internships: subset of placement companies
- Independent applications via LinkedIn/company portals: significant route
- Research internships at IITs/IISc: accessible for CGPA 8.0+ with faculty rec
- International research: DAAD WISE, MITACS, IUSSTF — accessible for strong profiles

## Realistic Interpretation

- CSE at IIT Mandi can lead to good software careers; not at IIT Delhi/Bombay level
- Students who do strong DSA prep + have good projects land ₹15–25 LPA roles reliably
- Top 5–10% of batch can access high-paying companies (₹30+ LPA)
- Bottom 20–30% may struggle or need to apply extensively to mid-tier companies
- Higher studies route (MS/PhD abroad, IIT M.Tech) is well-supported by IIT brand

## Year-Specific Risks

- 2022–23: Tech hiring boom; unusually high packages/placement %
- 2023–24: Tech industry slowdown; some offers deferred/reduced; placement % dropped
- 2024–25: Partial recovery expected but not confirmed
- Students should not base expectations on any single year

## What Differentiates Placed vs Unplaced/Lower-Package Students

- DSA preparation quality (LeetCode medium/hard; 200+ problems solved)
- Project quality (GitHub portfolio, competitive programming ratings)
- Internship experience in 3rd year
- Communication skills for HR/behavioral rounds
- CGPA (filter in some companies: 7.0+ or 7.5+)
