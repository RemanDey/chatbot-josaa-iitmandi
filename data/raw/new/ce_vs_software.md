# CE vs Software Careers at IIT Mandi

**Q: Can Civil Engineering students get software jobs?**

- Yes, but the transition is rare and very challenging. CE curriculum has almost no programming.
- Most software companies do NOT allow CE students for placements, but some service/IT companies may allow all branches.
- CE students who want software roles must self-learn all programming, DSA, and system design, and build a strong coding resume.

**Q: How hard is the transition?**

- Very hard. Requires exceptional self-motivation and early start (1st/2nd year).
- Success stories are rare but possible with strong coding contest performance and projects.

**Q: What are the main challenges?**

- Most companies filter out non-circuital branches (CE/EP/BS Chem).
- Competing with CSE/EE/DSE students who have much more coding experience.

**Tips:**
- Focus on coding contests, open-source, and internships.
- Seek mentorship from seniors who have made the transition.

**Summary:**
- Software transition from CE at IIT Mandi is possible but extremely rare and difficult. Most CE students pursue core, PSU, or higher studies paths.
