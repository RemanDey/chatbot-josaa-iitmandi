# CSE Student Profile at IIT Mandi

**Who succeeds in CSE?**
- Strong in math, logic, and programming
- Enjoys coding, algorithms, and problem-solving
- Self-motivated to learn new technologies
- Participates in coding contests, projects, and internships

**Who struggles?**
- Weak in math or uninterested in coding
- Avoids projects or self-study
- Not proactive in placements or internships

**Personality Fit:**
- Analytical, curious, self-driven
- Likes solving problems and building software

**Summary:**
- CSE is best for students who enjoy coding, algorithms, and continuous learning.
