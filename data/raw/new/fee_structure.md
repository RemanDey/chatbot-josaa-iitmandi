## 6. Special Cases & Clarifications

- **B.Tech–M.Tech Dual Degree:** M.Tech fee applicable from 5th year onwards.
- **B.Tech Double Major Programme:** Normal B.Tech fee continues in 5th year also.
- **Semester Break / Temporary Withdrawal / Maternity Leave:** ₹2,000 continuation fee per semester.
- **SC/ST/PwD:** 100% exemption on tuition fee only. Hostel, mess, and other charges still apply.
- **Mess Advance is approximate:** Actual billing done through vendor; excess refunded.
- **HP Students (Himachal Pradesh domicile):** May be eligible for HP government scholarship (separate from IIT Mandi scholarships).

## 7. Fee Payment Process

- Fees are paid through the **SAMARTH Portal** (academic portal of IIT Mandi).
- Payment schedule released at start of each semester.
- Late payment attracts penalty.
- For fee-related queries: Academics Section, IIT Mandi | academics@iitmandi.ac.in

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions — Fees

**Q: When is fee payment due?**  
A: At the beginning of each semester, as per the schedule on SAMARTH portal.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**  
A: Contact the Academics Section for installment/financial hardship cases.

**Q: How to get fee receipt?**  
A: Download from SAMARTH portal after payment.

**Q: Are fees the same every year?**  
A: Tuition fee is fixed per batch year; hostel/mess charges may be revised each semester.

**Q: I am SC/ST. Do I pay any fees?**  
A: Tuition fee is waived (100% exemption). You still pay hostel, mess, examination, and other charges.

**Q: What is Mess Advance? Will I get it back?**  
A: Mess Advance is pre-payment for mess food. Actual consumption is tracked; excess advance is refunded at semester end, shortfall is collected.

**Q: I want to take a semester break. What fees apply?**  
A: ₹2,000 per semester as continuation fee.
