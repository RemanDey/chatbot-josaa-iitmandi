# Math Intensity by Branch at IIT Mandi

**Most Math-Intensive:**
- MnC (Mathematics & Computing)
- EP (Engineering Physics)
- EE (Electrical Engineering)

**Moderate Math:**
- CSE/DSE (algorithms, ML, stats)
- ME (mechanics, design)
- MSE, Chem, Quantum (depends on electives)

**Least Math:**
- CE (Civil Engineering)
- BioE (Bio Engineering)
- IMBA (Integrated MBA)

**Summary:**
- Math intensity is highest in MnC, EP, and EE. CSE/DSE require strong math for algorithms/ML. CE and BioE are less math-heavy.
