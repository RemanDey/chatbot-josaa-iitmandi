# EE GATE and PSU Pathways at IIT Mandi

**PSU Optionality:**
- EE is the most PSU-aligned branch at IIT Mandi.
- Top PSUs: PowerGrid, NTPC, BHEL, IOCL, GAIL, HPCL, SAIL, etc.
- GATE EE rank under 1000 (often under 200) is needed for top PSUs.

**Preparation Tips:**
- Start GATE prep by 3rd year; use standard books and test series.
- Focus on core EE subjects: Power Systems, Machines, Control, Signals, Networks, Electronics.
- Many EE students at IIT Mandi succeed in GATE/PSU with focused effort.

**Summary:**
- EE is the best branch for PSU jobs via GATE at IIT Mandi. Consistent, early prep is key.
