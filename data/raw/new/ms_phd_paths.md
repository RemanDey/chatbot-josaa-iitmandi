# MS/PhD Paths after IIT Mandi B.Tech

**Overview:**
- Many IIT Mandi students pursue higher studies (MS/PhD) in India and abroad, especially from EP, EE, and core branches.

**Popular Destinations:**
- India: IITs, IISc, IISERs (via GATE, JAM, or direct interviews)
- Abroad: USA, Europe, Canada, Singapore (via GRE/TOEFL/IELTS, research internships, strong SOPs)

**Preparation Tips:**
- Build a strong academic record (CGPA, research projects, recommendations)
- Pursue research internships (SURGE, MITACS, DAAD, etc.)
- Start early (2nd/3rd year) for best results

**Summary:**
- Higher studies is a strong path for IIT Mandi students, especially in research-oriented branches.
