# Campus Life at IIT Mandi — Fact Sheet

Information about life at IIT Mandi campus, location, and connectivity.

## Location & Weather
- IIT Mandi is located in the scenic Kamand Valley of the Mandi district in Himachal Pradesh.
- The climate is very pleasant during the summer, rarely exceeding 30°C.
- Winters are cold, with temperatures frequently dropping to sub-zero levels. Frost and dew freezing are common.
- Direct snowfall on the campus is extremely rare, but hailstorms occur 3-4 times a year. Students often visit nearby snowy peaks like Prashar Lake or Kandi (about an hour away).

## Connectivity
- **Air:** The most practical airport is Chandigarh International Airport, located about 4.5 hours away by road. Cabs from the campus to Chandigarh cost approximately Rs. 4,500 for a 5-seater cab. Students frequently pool cabs to share costs.
- **Bus:** Traveling from Delhi or Chandigarh by Volvo bus is a popular and comfortable option. The buses drop students at Mandi town.
- **Local Commute:** IIT Mandi runs regular campus shuttle buses that transport students from Mandi town directly to the Kamand campus (about 30-40 minutes).

## Food and Hostels
- Hostels are equipped with functional hot water geysers, washing machines, refrigerators, microwaves, induction cooktops, and 24/7 automated snack vending machines.
- First-year students share quad rooms with bunk beds, while single/double rooms are allocated from the second year onwards based on CGPA.
- There are over 10 active canteens and eateries on campus. External delivery services like Swiggy and Zomato are not available due to the remote location.

## Student Life
- The campus has high-speed Wi-Fi across all hostels and academic blocks.
- Jio and Airtel provide the most reliable mobile network coverage in the Kamand Valley.
- There are numerous active technical and cultural clubs, and excellent sports facilities for badminton, table tennis, football, cricket, squash, and basketball.
