# EE Student Profile at IIT Mandi

**Who succeeds in EE?**
- Strong in math and physics
- Enjoys circuits, electronics, and problem-solving
- Willing to put in long hours for labs and projects
- Interested in both hardware and software

**Who struggles?**
- Weak in math or uninterested in hardware
- Avoids labs or hands-on work
- Not self-motivated for GATE/PSU or placements

**Personality Fit:**
- Analytical, detail-oriented, persistent
- Likes both theory and practical work

**Summary:**
- EE is best for students who enjoy math, circuits, and a mix of hardware/software challenges.
