# EE vs DSE at IIT Mandi

**EE (Electrical Engineering):**
- Core engineering, circuits, power, VLSI
- Best for PSU/core/government jobs
- Software transition possible, but needs DSA prep

**DSE (Data Science & Engineering):**
- Focus on data, ML, AI, statistics
- Best for software/data science roles
- PSU/core jobs rare

**Summary:**
- Choose EE for core/PSU/government, DSE for software/data science. Both are competitive, but career paths differ.
