# IIT Mandi — Student Life, Clubs, Placement & Facilities
**Source:** IIT Mandi Student Affairs Section, Dean Students Office, CnP Cell  
**Contact:** deanstudentsoffice@iitmandi.ac.in | cnpcell@iitmandi.ac.in

---

## 1. Campus Life Overview

IIT Mandi is located in a scenic valley in Himachal Pradesh with extreme variation in climate — hot summers, heavy monsoons, cold winters with snowfall. The campus is about 14 km from Mandi town, on the banks of Uhl River at Kamand.

- Fully residential campus
- Strong community and student life despite remote location
- Active student bodies: Gymkhana, technical clubs, cultural societies

---

## 2. Gymkhana — Student Activities Body

The **Gymkhana** is the umbrella body for all student activities at IIT Mandi.

### Hierarchy:
1. Dean (Students) — top authority
2. Chairperson-Students Activity Planning (Chair-SAP)
3. Faculty Advisor — Society
4. Faculty Advisor — Club
5. Student coordinators

### 2.1 Societies

| Society | Publication |
|---|---|
| Technical Society | Essent Tech Magazine |
| Literary Society | Vivaan Literary Magazine |
| Cultural Society | Events, fests |
| Sports Society | Sports events, inter-IIT |

### 2.2 Clubs

IIT Mandi has a wide range of clubs covering:
- Technical (coding, robotics, electronics, AI/ML)
- Arts and culture
- Music
- Photography
- Trekking and adventure
- Quizzing
- Drama

**How to join:** Approach the respective society/club. No formal application process for most; just show up and participate.

**How to participate in competitions outside IIT Mandi:**
- Approach the relevant society/club
- Institute has a provision to facilitate outside participation
- Funding/permission granted through society → faculty advisor → Dean (Students)

## 3. Career and Placement (CnP) Cell

**Website:** placements.iitmandi.ac.in  
**Contact:** cnpcell@iitmandi.ac.in

### 3.1 Placement Overview

- Over 100+ companies recruit from IIT Mandi
- Sectors: IT, Core Engineering, Analytics, Finance, Consulting, Research
- B.Tech CSE has the highest demand and packages

### 3.2 CnP Policies

| Document | Description |
|---|---|
| CnP Policy for Students | Rules students must follow during placement season |
| Placement Policy | How placement process works |
| Internship Policy | Rules for summer/winter internships |

### 3.3 Internship Process

- Internship policy updated for 2025-26 (document: Internship_Policy.pdf on CnP cell portal)
- Summer internships: Apply through CnP cell for company-based internships
- Research internships (SRFP, SURGE, MITACS): Apply independently

### 3.4 School of Management (SOM) Placements

- SOM has its own Training and Placement committee for MBA students
- Institute-level CnP cell also supports SOM students
- Coordinate via both SOM T&P and CnP cell

## 5. Health & Medical Facilities

- **Health Center:** On campus
- **Guidance and Counselling Service (GCS):** Mental health and counselling support
- **Medical coverage:** Included in annual fees (₹750/year)
- **Contact for counselling:** Through Dean Students office | deanstudentsoffice@iitmandi.ac.in

> **Mental health matters.** If you're struggling, reach out to GCS. All sessions are confidential.

## 6. Transportation & How to Reach

### Campus Location
- Kamand, Mandi District, Himachal Pradesh — 175001
- 14 km from Mandi town
- Nearest railway station: Joginder Nagar (~60 km)
- Nearest airport: Bhuntar (Kullu-Manali Airport) (~70 km) / Gaggal Airport (Kangra) (~80 km)

### From Mandi Town to Campus
- Institute buses / shared taxis available
- Auto-rickshaws available from Mandi bus stand
- Check with hostel secretary for latest bus schedule

### Bus Service (Within Campus / Town)
- Institute bus service between North and South campuses
- Buses to Mandi town on scheduled timings
- Check with Dean Students office or hostel notice board for current schedule

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions — Student Life

**Q: How do I join a club or society at IIT Mandi?**  
A: Approach the respective society or club directly. Most clubs hold inductions/tryouts at the start of each semester. No formal application needed for most.

**Q: Can I participate in competitions/hackathons outside IIT Mandi?**  
A: Yes. Approach the relevant society/club, get approval through faculty advisor → Dean (Students). Institute provides support for external participation.

**Q: Is there a counselling service on campus?**  
A: Yes. Guidance and Counselling Service (GCS) provides mental health support, career guidance, and personal counselling. Reach out through deanstudentsoffice@iitmandi.ac.in.

**Q: How far is the campus from Mandi town? How do I travel?**  
A: About 14 km. Institute buses and shared taxis are available. Check hostel notice board for current bus schedules.

**Q: Can my parents visit and where do they stay?**  
A: Parents are welcome. Guest House is available on campus. Contact infraoffice@iitmandi.ac.in for booking. Parents cannot stay in student hostels.

**Q: Is there a placement cell? Which companies come?**  
A: Yes, Career and Placement (CnP) cell is active. 100+ companies recruit across all branches in IT, core engineering, analytics, consulting, and research.

**Q: I need a sports facility. What's available?**  
A: Sports grounds, indoor games in hostels, and sports societies are active. Inquire with Gymkhana sports society for facilities list and inter-IIT sports team selection.

**Q: Is there a startup culture at IIT Mandi?**  
A: Yes. IIT Mandi Catalyst is the startup incubator on campus. iHub is a technology innovation hub. Several student startups have been incubated.
