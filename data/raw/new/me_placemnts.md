# IIT Mandi ME — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 66.67% | 13.36 LPA | 12 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 96% | 15.96 LPA | 14.1 LPA |

## Software vs Core Split

- Software roles (ME transitioning): ~25–35% of placed ME students
- Core ME roles (manufacturing, automotive, aerospace): ~35–45%
- PSU/government: ~10–20%
- Higher studies/GATE: ~15–25% of batch (not counted in placement %)

## Key Recruiters — Core ME Track

- L&T (construction/manufacturing divisions)
- Tata Motors (engineering roles)
- Maruti Suzuki (manufacturing, quality)
- BHEL (through GATE primarily)
- HAL (Hindustan Aeronautics Limited)
- DRDO (research roles)
- ISRO (mechanical/aerospace)
- Oil & gas companies: ONGC, IOCL civil/mechanical (GATE route)

## Key Recruiters — Software Track (ME Transitioning)

- Same software companies as other branches; ME students face higher competition
- Need equivalent DSA prep; less natural transition

## PSU Route

- GATE ME: significant ME student investment
- Target: BHEL, NTPC (mechanical), IOCL, ONGC, GAIL, SAIL
- GATE ME competition: large candidate pool (one of the larger GATE papers by volume)
- Success rate at IIT Mandi: individual-dependent; IIT brand helps marginally

## Realistic Interpretation

- ME is the toughest placement environment at IIT Mandi
- Core ME starting salaries (₹6–10 LPA for manufacturing) are lower than software
- Software transition requires maximum effort; not guaranteed
- PSU is the most realistic stable career path for ME students
- Students with specific ME interest (robotics, aerospace) have better placement stories
- Higher studies abroad (robotics, manufacturing systems) is legitimate pathway

## Critical Honest Assessment

- ME at a remote IIT without strong manufacturing industry nearby is a hard proposition
- Students who entered ME only as "last resort at IIT" face real career challenges
- Students with genuine mechanical interest + GATE/higher studies plan: reasonable outcome
