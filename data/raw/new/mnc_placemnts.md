# IIT Mandi MnC — Placement Details

## Critical Caveat

MnC batch is very small (20–30 students). Data is insufficient for reliable statistics. All estimates have very low confidence.

## Estimated Metrics

| Metric | Estimate | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| Placement % (of registered) | 75–90% | Very Low |
| Median CTC | ₹16–22 LPA | Very Low |
| Highest CTC | ₹35–60 LPA | Very Low |

## Career Distribution

- Software engineering (SDE): ~40–50%
- Data science/ML: ~20–25%
- Quant finance (rare but notable): ~5–10%
- Higher studies: ~20–30%

## Key Recruiters

- Same software companies as CSE
- Analytics firms
- Quant finance firms (Jane Street, WorldQuant — rare; possible for top students)
- Research institutions

## Honest Assessment

- MnC students who do adequate DSA prep place similarly to CSE students in SWE roles
- Math strength gives edge in data science, ML interviews
- Quant finance is possible but highly competitive; IIT Mandi brand is a small negative vs IIT Delhi/Bombay for quant firms
- Higher studies from MnC: very well supported (math background is premium for PhD programs in CS/Applied Math)
