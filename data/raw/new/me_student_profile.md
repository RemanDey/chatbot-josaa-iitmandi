# ME Student Profile at IIT Mandi

**Who succeeds in ME?**
- Strong in physics, mechanics, and design
- Enjoys hands-on work, labs, and projects
- Interested in GATE/PSU or core engineering roles

**Who struggles?**
- Weak in math/physics or uninterested in core engineering
- Avoids labs or field work
- Not self-motivated for placements or higher studies

**Personality Fit:**
- Practical, hands-on, persistent
- Likes building, designing, and problem-solving

**Summary:**
- ME is best for students who enjoy mechanics, design, and practical engineering challenges.
