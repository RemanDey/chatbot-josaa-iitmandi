# IIT Mandi EE — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 61.66% | 17.75 LPA | 14 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 93% | 24.58 LPA | 19.75 LPA |

## Software vs Core Split

- Software roles (EE students transitioning): ~40–55% of placed EE students
- Core EE roles (VLSI, embedded, power, semiconductors): ~25–35%
- PSU/government (through GATE): ~10–15%
- Research/higher studies (not counted as "placements"): ~15–25% of batch

## Key Recruiters — Core EE Track

- Qualcomm (VLSI/signal processing): visits occasionally; competitive
- Texas Instruments (digital/analog design): occasional
- Samsung Semiconductor (R&D Noida/Bangalore): occasional
- Power Grid, NTPC, BHEL: primarily through GATE route, not direct campus
- Bosch, Continental: embedded systems roles (automotive)
- ISRO, DRDO: research roles; competitive

## Key Recruiters — Software Track (EE students)

- Same software companies as CSE but through same placement process
- EE students compete against CSE/DSE; need equivalent DSA prep

## PSU Route

- GATE EE preparation: significant number of IIT Mandi EE students pursue GATE
- Target PSUs: PGCIL (Power Grid), NTPC, BHEL, IOCL, GAIL, HPCL (EE category)
- GATE scores required for top PSUs: typically rank <1000 in GATE EE for top PSUs
- IIT Mandi EE students who prepare seriously do qualify; not at high rates compared to older IITs

## Realistic Interpretation

- EE students who want software must put in DSA prep equivalent to CSE students
- Core EE placement is present but smaller volume than software; VLSI/embedded companies are selective
- PSU route is reliable safety net; GATE preparation success is individual-dependent
- Higher studies (GATE M.Tech or MS abroad): significant fraction of EE students
- Total expected outcome range: 40–60% in software, 20–30% in core/PSU, 20% higher studies/other

## PPO Discussion

- PPOs less common in EE than CSE
- Core EE internship PPOs possible from semiconductor/embedded companies
- Software PPOs for EE students who prep adequately: possible
