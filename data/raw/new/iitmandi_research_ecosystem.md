# IIT Mandi — Research Ecosystem

## Overall Research Output

- IIT Mandi has growing research output; publications in Scopus/Web of Science indexed journals increasing
- Some faculty with strong international collaborations
- NIRF research score: moderate (better than many NITs, below older IITs)
- Total research funding: tens of crores across active projects (SERB, DST, MeitY grants)
- Honest caveat: Research depth and lab infrastructure is not comparable to IIT Madras, IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi

## Key Research Areas

### Computer Science & Engineering
- Machine Learning, Deep Learning
- Computer Vision
- Natural Language Processing (some work)
- High-Performance Computing
- Human-Computer Interaction (linked to iHub)
- IoT and Embedded Systems

### Electrical Engineering
- Signal Processing

- Smart Grid and Energy Systems

### Mechanical Engineering

## IIT Mandi iHub and HCI Foundation

- Established under NM-ICPS (National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems)
- Focus: Human-Computer Interaction, Cyber-Physical Systems, AI for mountain communities
- Provides funded projects and internships for students
- Some startup incubation under iHub
- Honest assessment: iHub is a genuine initiative but early-stage; deliverables are still being built

## Research Opportunities for UG Students

- Faculty-guided B.Tech projects (mandatory final year)
- Summer research placements under faculty (self-arranged)
- Some SERB-funded projects take UG interns with stipends (~5,000–10,000/month)
- Research paper co-authorship possible for motivated UG students (happens occasionally)
- Recommendation letters for higher studies: IIT Mandi faculty letters are respected (IIT brand matters)

## Research Infrastructure — Honest

- Core labs functional: Electronics labs, Mechanical workshop, Materials characterization (XRD, SEM — some equipment)
- Computing resources: Some GPU clusters available for ML research; not at IIT Madras/IIT Bombay scale
- Library: Digital access (Knimbus/NDL/Shodhganga) adequate; physical library growing
- Research clusters: Faculty research groups exist; some have 3–8 PhD students per group
- Startup incubator: Catalyst (IIT Mandi startup incubator) — early stage

## PhD and MS Programs

- PhD stipend: ₹37,000–42,000/month (MHRD rates, standard across IITs)
- MS (Research): 2-year program with thesis; good pathway for IIT brand + research at lower competition
- M.Tech: 2-year taught program; some with GATE fellowship
- External PhD program: Available for working professionals

## Higher Studies Pipeline from IIT Mandi

- Students going to IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, IIT Madras for M.Tech/PhD: happens regularly
- Students going abroad (MS/PhD at US/European universities): smaller numbers but happens
- IIT brand on CV helps significantly for MS applications even from IIT Mandi
- GATE performance: Mixed; students do qualify but not at high rates compared to older IIT students
- GRE/TOEFL prep: Individual student effort; no institutionally organized support

## Honest Research Positioning

- IIT Mandi is a legitimate research institution but at an early growth stage
- Faculty are publishing but impact factor and citation counts vary
- Students who want to do serious research are better off at IIT Madras/IIT Bombay/IIT Delhi if they have the rank
- Students who cannot access those but want IIT-quality mentorship for higher studies: IIT Mandi is a reasonable choice
- The Himalayan location creates unique research opportunities in disaster management, environmental science, HCI for mountain communities
