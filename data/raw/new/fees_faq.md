# Fees and Scholarships at IIT Mandi — Fact Sheet

Verified information regarding the fee structure, waivers, and scholarships for B.Tech students.

## General Fee Structure
- **Tuition Fee:** The institute fee for B.Tech students in the UR, OBC, and EWS categories is approximately Rs. 1,01,000 for the even semester.
- **Hostel Charges:** The approximate hostel room rent/charge is Rs. 9,600 per semester.
- **Mess Charges:** The approximate mess advance/charge is Rs. 15,360 per semester.

## Waivers and Concessions
- **SC/ST/PwD Students:** All B.Tech and BS students belonging to the SC, ST, and PwD categories are granted a 100% tuition fee waiver as per Government of India regulations.
- **Female Students:** To promote women in engineering, female students admitted to the undergraduate programs receive a 100% tuition fee waiver along with a monthly stipend of Rs. 1,000.

## Scholarships
- **Merit-cum-Means (MCM) Scholarship:** This scholarship is designed to support meritorious students from economically weaker sections. It provides tuition fee waivers and stipends based on academic performance (CGPA) and family income limits.
