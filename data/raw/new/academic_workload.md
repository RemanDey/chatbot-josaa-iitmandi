# Academic Workload by Branch at IIT Mandi

**Highest Workload:**
- EE (labs, theory, projects)
- EP (research, theory, labs)
- CSE/DSE (projects, coding, assignments)

**Moderate Workload:**
- ME (labs, design, GATE/PSU prep)
- MnC (math, coding, projects)
- MSE, Chem, Quantum (new, evolving)

**Lower Workload:**
- CE (field work, core focus)
- BioE (interdisciplinary, less lab load)
- IMBA (management, less technical)

**Summary:**
- EE/EP/CSE/DSE have the highest academic workload. CE/BioE/IMBA are less demanding in terms of assignments and lab hours.
