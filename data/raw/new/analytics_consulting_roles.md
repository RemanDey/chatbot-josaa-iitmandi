# Analytics & Consulting Roles at IIT Mandi

**Analytics Companies:**
- EXL Analytics, WNS, Mu Sigma, ZS Associates, etc.

**Consulting Firms:**
- McKinsey, BCG, Bain (rare, mostly via off-campus)
- Big 4 (Deloitte, EY, KPMG, PwC)

**Eligibility:**
- CSE/DSE: All
- EE/ME/EP: Some
- CE/BioE: Few

**Summary:**
- Analytics/consulting roles are open to CSE/DSE and some circuital branches. Top consulting firms are rare on campus; most students apply off-campus.
