# EP vs Software Careers at IIT Mandi

**Q: Can Engineering Physics students get software jobs?**

- Yes, but the transition is challenging. EP curriculum is math/physics-heavy, with little formal programming.
- Some software/analytics companies allow EP students, but most top product companies filter by branch.
- EP students who want software roles must self-learn DSA, algorithms, and system design, and build a strong coding resume.

**Q: How hard is the transition?**

- Hard. Requires strong self-motivation and early start (1st/2nd year).
- Some EP students have succeeded, especially in analytics/data science roles.

**Q: What are the main challenges?**

- Competing with CSE/EE/DSE students who have more coding experience.
- Branch-based filtering in many companies.

**Tips:**
- Focus on coding contests, analytics, and internships.
- Build a strong project portfolio and seek mentorship.

**Summary:**
- Software transition from EP at IIT Mandi is possible, especially for analytics/data science, but is less common than from circuital branches.
