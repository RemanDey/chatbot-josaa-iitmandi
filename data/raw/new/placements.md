## Career and Placement Cell (CnP Cell)

The Career and Placement Cell helps students in two ways:
1. Spreads awareness about career paths and prepares students through guidance sessions and workshops.
2. Connects the institute with industry by inviting companies for internships and full-time hiring.

The CnP Cell organizes campus internship and placement drives annually, with companies from multiple domains recruiting students.

## Year-to-Year Variation

Placements vary with:
- Economic cycle and hiring conditions
- Recruiter participation each year
- Student preparation and cohort mix
