# EE vs Software Careers at IIT Mandi

**Q: Can Electrical Engineering students get software jobs?**

- Yes, but the transition requires strong DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms) prep and self-driven coding projects.
- Most software companies at IIT Mandi allow EE students to sit for placements, but some top product companies may filter by CGPA or branch.
- EE students who want software roles should start coding early (1st/2nd year), participate in coding clubs, and build a strong resume (LeetCode, GitHub, internships).

**Q: How hard is the transition?**

- Moderate. EE covers some programming, but not as much as CSE/DSE. You must self-study DSA and system design.
- Many EE students at IIT Mandi have successfully placed in software roles, but the top packages are rare compared to CSE.

**Q: What are the main challenges?**

- Competing with CSE/DSE students who have more coursework in algorithms and software engineering.
- Some companies require branch-specific eligibility (check each year’s placement brochure).

**Tips:**
- Join coding clubs, do internships, and participate in hackathons.
- Prepare for online assessments (OA) and interviews with a focus on DSA and problem-solving.

**Summary:**
- Software transition is possible from EE at IIT Mandi, but requires extra effort and early preparation.
