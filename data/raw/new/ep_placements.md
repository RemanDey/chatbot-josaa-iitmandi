# IIT Mandi EP — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.
- EP batch size is small; year-to-year variation can be large.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 29.41% | 9.83 LPA | 9 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 79% | 20.9 LPA | 14.1 LPA |

## Career Distribution

- Higher studies (primary path): PhD at IITs/IISc/abroad; MS abroad
- Software roles (those who transition): SDE or data science
- Research labs: BARC, DRDO, ISRO (small numbers)
- Quant finance (rare): possible for top EP students
- Semiconductor/photonics companies: niche placements

## Honest Assessment

- EP is primarily a higher-studies feeder branch
- Industry placement data is unreliable due to small sample
- Students entering EP expecting industry placements comparable to CSE will be disappointed
- Top EP students can access quant finance, semiconductor research, ML research roles
- BARC OCES pathway: legitimate route for EP graduates (competitive but direct)

## Research Career Pipeline

- TIFR, BARC, IISc, IUCAA: natural PhD destinations
- IIT Mandi faculty research provides strong PhD recommendation letters
- Abroad PhD at US/European universities in condensed matter, photonics, materials
