## Computer Science and Data Science Branches
- **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE):** Features an advanced curriculum covering algorithms, systems, software engineering, and artificial intelligence. Backed by a strong coding culture and excellent placement statistics.
- **Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI):** Focuses heavily on statistics, machine learning, deep learning, data visualization, and AI. Placement opportunities and average packages are highly comparable to the CSE branch.
- **Mathematics and Computing (MnC):** Provides a rigorous foundation in advanced mathematics and computer science. It requires 160 credits and is highly valued for roles in quantitative finance, algorithms, cryptography, and investment banking.

## Circuital Branches
- **Electrical Engineering (EE):** A well-established core branch covering power systems, controls, robotics, and electronics. The coursework is demanding, but it has a proven track record of excellent placements and higher education opportunities.
- **Microelectronics & VLSI:** Listed as a dedicated B.Tech program focused on integrated circuits, semiconductor physics, and chip design. Refer to official curriculum documents for current course details.

## Emerging and Interdisciplinary Programs

- **General Engineering (GE):** A flexible B.Tech program with an exploration-first structure followed by specialization choices.
- **Chemical Engineering and Data Analytics:** Program listed in IIT Mandi sources; see curriculum PDF in data/raw/new for details.
- **Agricultural Engineering with Data Analytics:** Program listed in IIT Mandi sources; verify curriculum details in institute documents.
- **Quantum Science and Engineering:** Program listed in IIT Mandi sources; see curriculum PDF in data/raw/new for details.

## Core and Applied Branches
- **Mechanical Engineering:** Features a rigorous curriculum covering core mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing. The branch achieves solid core placements, and students frequently utilize free time to take software electives.
- **Civil Engineering:** Focuses on infrastructure, structural design, and environmental engineering.
- **Engineering Physics (EP):** Combines rigorous foundations in quantum physics and field theory with engineering disciplines. It is a highly flexible branch, giving students time to pursue minors or coding.
- **Bio Engineering:** Interdisciplinary bioengineering program offered as a B.Tech-M.Tech dual degree.
- **Materials Science and Engineering (MSE):** Focuses on semiconductor materials, advanced metallurgy, product design, and materials informatics.

## Science Programs
- **BS Chemical Sciences:** A 4-year undergraduate research-oriented program requiring 163 total credits. It includes major research projects in the 7th and 8th semesters and teaches Python in the very first semester.

## Management Program
- **Integrated MBA (IMBA):** 5-year integrated BS + MBA program offered via the School of Management with a separate admission process.
