# IIT Mandi — Hostel Rules & Regulations
**Source:** Official Hostel Rules Document, IIT Mandi (Updated: November 2023 / August 2024)  
**Authority:** Dean (Students), Chief Warden, IIT Mandi  
**Contact:** deanstudentsoffice@iitmandi.ac.in

---

## 1. Accommodation & Allotment

- IIT Mandi is a **fully residential campus**. All B.Tech, M.Sc., MA, MBA students are required to stay in hostels.
- PhD and M.Tech (Research) scholars may request off-campus stay, subject to approval.
- Room allotment is done by hostel administration at the start of each semester.
- Students cannot choose specific rooms; allotment is administrative.
- Students are allotted a hostel; they **cannot change their hostel** without Chief Warden approval.

## 3. Mess Rules

### 3.1 Mess Allocation

- At the beginning of each semester, hostel admin/secretary may ask for mess preference.
- Due to dining area space constraints, hostel admin will allocate a mess which could be different from individual preference.
- **Once allocated, students cannot change their mess for the entire semester.**
- Mess change requests are accepted via SAMARTH portal or Google Form, circulated **once every 2 months**. Approval of Chief Warden is required.

### 3.2 Mess-Off (Temporary Rebate)

**You can get mess-off ONLY for the following reasons:**
1. Mid-semester break (institute holidays)
2. Institute-related official work
3. Personal leave as sanctioned by respective authorities
4. Medical leave (with valid medical certificate)

**Procedure:**
- Submit request with proper justification through **SAMARTH portal**
- Approval from **Chief Warden** is required
- Mess-off days counted only as per duly approved hostel vacate form
- **No other reason is accepted for mess-off**

> **No mess-off is given for:**
> - Attending events/fests outside campus without official leave
> - Self-decided absence from campus
> - Vacations not officially sanctioned

### 3.3 Mess Rules — Discipline

- **Food will not be served in rooms.** Hostellers must eat in the mess dining hall.
- **Taking mess utensils or food to rooms is prohibited.** Exception: hostellers who are ill (with hostel management's permission).
- Any complaints/suggestions about food quality or hygiene: email deanstudentsoffice@iitmandi.ac.in or contact Warden/Asst. Warden/General Sec/Mess Coordinator.
- **Canteen services are separate from mess.** Cannot use canteen in place of mess or vice versa.

## 4. Hostel Leave & Outings

### 4.1 Leave Form Requirement

- **All students (including PhD)** must submit a hostel leaving/intimation form if proceeding outside campus (hometown, personal visit, official visit).
- Purpose of leave must be stated clearly. Writing only "personal" is **not acceptable**.

### 4.2 Restrictions for Students Under 18 and Junior Students

- BTech, MSc, MA, MBA, MTech(R) students **and** students under 18 years of age must:
  - Seek **written permission from the warden**
  - Parent/guardian acknowledgement is required
  - Required for going out of campus or beyond Mandi town at any time (day or night)

### 4.3 Mid-Semester Vacations

- Students who vacate the hostel mid-semester and are later relieved from the institute must still pay hostel charges on a **pro-rata basis** for their actual stay period.

## 5. General Conduct & Discipline

### 5.1 Expected Behavior

- All hostellers must maintain standards of conduct expected of students of a National Importance institution.
- Courteous and fair behavior with everyone inside and outside hostel premises is mandatory.
- **Always carry valid Institute ID card.** Must be produced when asked by any official.

### 5.2 Prohibited Items & Activities

**Strictly Prohibited:**
- Electrical appliances in rooms: immersion heaters, electric stoves, electric irons (separate from iron provided in common room)
- Cooking inside hostel rooms
- Alcohol, narcotics, gambling
- Ragging in any form

**Other Prohibitions:**
- Hiring external persons to clean rooms (students must clean their own rooms)
- Taking mess utensils/food to rooms
- Damaging hostel property (willful damage attracts penalty)
- Drunken or riotous behavior

### 5.3 Anti-Ragging Policy (Zero Tolerance)

- **Expulsion from Institute** is imposed on any student found involved in ragging. No mercy plea will be considered.
- Individuals may be handed over to law enforcement agencies.
- **Any fresher or resident subjected to ragging MUST immediately report to:**
  - Wardens
  - Resident Wardens
  - Chief Warden
- Anti-ragging helpline available on campus.

|---|---|
| Director, IIT Mandi | Final authority on all hostel matters |

## 7. Maintenance & Cleanliness

- Both hostel management and residents are responsible for cleanliness of hostel premises including building, courtyards, and toilets.
- Students must clean their own rooms.
- Hostel property must be kept in good condition.
- Damage to hostel property will result in fine and/or disciplinary action.

## 9. Semester End — Vacating Hostel

- At end of each semester, students must vacate their rooms as per schedule.
- Room must be left clean and in proper condition.
- Return all hostel keys to caretaker.
- Failure to vacate in time: additional charges may apply.
- Students interning for 90 days or more must vacate hostel and surrender room (cannot retain room while on long internship).

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions — Hostels

**Q: Is hostel compulsory for B.Tech students?**  
A: Yes. IIT Mandi is a fully residential campus. B.Tech students must stay in hostels.

**Q: Can I live off-campus?**  
A: Only PhD/MTech(R) scholars may request off-campus accommodation, subject to approval.

**Q: Can I choose my room or roommate?**  
A: No. Room allotment is done by hostel administration.

**Q: What should I bring? Does IIT Mandi provide mattress?**  
A: Bed frame is provided, but mattress is NOT provided. Bring your own mattress/bedding.

**Q: How do I get mess-off?**  
A: Apply on SAMARTH portal with justification. Chief Warden approval required. Only valid for official leaves/medical.

**Q: Can I cook in my room?**  
A: No. Cooking in rooms and electrical appliances (heaters, stoves) are strictly prohibited. Common room has microwave and induction.

**Q: Who do I contact for hostel maintenance issues?**  
A: Contact your hostel caretaker first. Escalate to Warden/Resident Warden if unresolved.

**Q: What happens if I violate hostel rules?**  
A: Penalties range from fines to disciplinary action to expulsion, depending on severity.

**Q: I was ragged. What do I do?**  
A: Report IMMEDIATELY to Warden, Resident Warden, or Chief Warden. Zero tolerance policy — strict action guaranteed.

**Q: Can my parents stay in the hostel when they visit?**  
A: No. Guest House is available on campus for parents. Visitors cannot stay in student hostels.

**Q: I want to change my hostel. Is that possible?**  
A: Hostel changes require Chief Warden approval and are generally only allowed for valid reasons.
