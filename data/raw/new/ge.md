# IIT Mandi - General Engineering (GE)

## 1. Program Overview
- Degree: B.Tech
- Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
- Admission: JEE Advanced via JoSAA
- School: School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering (SMME)

## 2. Confirmed Program Structure
- The first year is a broad, interdisciplinary foundation across engineering areas.
- From the second year onward, students choose a specialization track.

## 3. Specialization Options (Current Sources)
- AI and Robotics
- Mechatronics and AI
- Communications Technology
- Open Specialisation
- Fintech specialization is also listed in institute data
