# IIT Mandi CE — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 31.25% | 10.45 LPA | 9 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 71% | 12.65 LPA | 10.95 LPA |

## Placement Category Distribution

- Core civil/infrastructure roles: ~40–55%
- Government/PSU (GATE route): ~20–30%
- Higher studies (M.Tech, MS): ~15–25%
- Software (very limited): ~5–10%

## Key Recruiters — Core CE

- L&T Construction
- Shapoorji Pallonji
- IRCON International
- RITES Limited
- NBCC, WAPCOS
- Infrastructure consulting firms

## PSU Route (CE)

- GATE CE → AAI, ONGC civil, NHPC, NHAI, CPWD
- IES (Indian Engineering Services) exam: civil engineering category
- Central government engineering positions: PWD, irrigation departments
- IES is prestigious but extremely competitive; selection rate ~0.1%

## Realistic Interpretation

- CE placement outcomes are lowest among IIT Mandi branches in private sector
- Government service / PSU is the most viable path for CE students
- Infrastructure sector CTC (₹7–12 LPA for L&T type companies) is decent for non-metro cost of living
- IIT Mandi CE brand primarily helps for: government selection, IIT M.Tech admission
- Software pivot essentially impossible without extraordinary independent effort
- Research in disaster management / geotechnical: niche but genuine strength at IIT Mandi CE
