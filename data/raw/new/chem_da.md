# IIT Mandi - Chemical Engineering and Data Analytics

## 1. Program Overview
- Degree: B.Tech
- Admission: JEE Advanced via JoSAA
- Status: Listed among IIT Mandi UG programs in institute sources

## 2. Available Curriculum Source
- A curriculum PDF is available in data/raw/new: "Chemical Engineering with Data Analytics Course Curricullum.pdf".
- OCR text was extracted from the PDF for curriculum details. Minor OCR errors are possible; verify exact course titles in the PDF.

## 3. Curriculum Snapshot (OCR from Official PDF)

### Foundation and Core Courses (Examples)
- Introduction to Python and Data Science
- Applied Electronics (with lab)
- Engineering Graphics
- Introduction to Chemical Engineering
- Probability and Statistics
- Applied Chemistry for Engineers
- Foundations of Design Practicum
- Physics Practicum

### Chemical Engineering Core (Examples)
- Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics
- Chemical Reaction Engineering I and II
- Heat Transfer
- Mass Transfer
- Fluid Mechanics
- Numerical Methods
- Transport Phenomenon
- Process Control and Instrumentation
- Chemical Process Technology
- Process Equipment Design
- Chemical Engineering Lab (remaining experiments)
- Climate Tech and Sustainable Process

### Data Analytics in Chemical Engineering (Tentative Topics)
- AI for process industries (advanced process control, CV for inspection, NLP for process systems)
- IoT and sensor networks for chemical plants
- Digital twin modeling and predictive maintenance
- Smart manufacturing and Industry 4.0
- Machine learning for green chemistry
- AI-driven sustainable process design and decarbonization
- Carbon capture and storage, renewable energy systems modeling
- Circular economy and process integration
- Molecular simulation methods and generative AI for materials design
- Polymer informatics and soft matter modeling
- AI for catalysis and surface science
- Multiscale modeling of materials
- Formulation science and engineering
- Time series analysis and forecasting
- Bayesian methods in engineering
- Optimization algorithms for engineering
- Graph neural networks for molecular systems
- Cloud computing and scalable data pipelines
- Database systems and management

**Credit note (from PDF):** Students need to complete a total of 21 credits from Data Analytics in ChE courses (discipline electives and/or free electives).
