# Branch Difficulty Comparison at IIT Mandi

**Relative Difficulty (Typical):**
- CSE/DSE: High (coding, algorithms, projects, competitive placements)
- EE: Very High (math, circuits, labs, core + software)
- EP: Very High (math, physics, research, less placement focus)
- ME: High (mechanics, design, labs, GATE/PSU focus)
- CE: Moderate-High (core engineering, field work, GATE/PSU focus)
- MnC: Very High (math rigor, algorithms, quant)
- BioE, MSE, Chem, Agri, Quantum: Moderate-High (interdisciplinary, new programs)

**Notes:**
- All branches require consistent effort; difficulty is subjective.
- EE/EP/MnC are most math-intensive.
- CSE/DSE are most competitive for placements.
- ME/CE are core-heavy, with more field/lab work.

**Summary:**
- Choose based on your strengths and interests, not just perceived difficulty.
