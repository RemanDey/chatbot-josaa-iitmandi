# Branch vs College Tradeoff — IIT Mandi Counseling

**Top Indian counseling question:**
- Should I choose a better branch at a newer IIT (e.g., IIT Mandi CSE) or a core branch at an older IIT (e.g., IIT Patna EE)?

**Key Factors:**
- Branch interest and career goals (software, core, research, management)
- Placement statistics (median/average CTC, % placed)
- Brand value (old IITs have stronger alumni, core jobs; new IITs have better software/data focus)
- Location and campus life
- Research and higher studies opportunities

**Example Scenarios:**
- IIT Mandi EE vs IIT Bombay/Delhi/Patna EE: Old IITs have stronger core/PSU pipeline, but Mandi may offer better software/data roles.
- IIT Mandi CSE vs NIT Trichy CSE: IIT brand helps for higher studies, but NITs may have better core/industry connections.
- Mandi CSE vs Patna EE: CSE at Mandi is better for software; EE at Patna is better for core/PSU.

**Summary:**
- There is no universal answer. Choose branch if you have a clear career goal (software, research, core). Choose college if you value brand, alumni, and core/PSU jobs. Always check latest placement/cutoff data and talk to seniors.
