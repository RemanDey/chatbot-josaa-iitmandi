# 1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

## What You Study at IIT Mandi

**Years 1–2 (Foundation):**
- Programming (C/C++, Python, Java)
- Data Structures and Algorithms
- Discrete Mathematics
- Digital Logic and Computer Organization
- Linear Algebra, Calculus, Probability & Statistics
- Object-Oriented Programming

**Years 3–4 (Core + Electives):**
- Theory of Computation
- Operating Systems
- Computer Networks
- Compiler Design
- Database Management Systems
- Software Engineering
- Machine Learning
- Electives from CAIR lab: Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity

**IIT Mandi Advantage:** Direct access to the **Center for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (CAIR)** for UG research projects. Strong faculty in ML, signal processing, and robotics.

## Real Placement Data (IIT Mandi)

| Year | Placed | Avg Package | Median | Highest |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 | 89.32% | ₹26.55 LPA | ₹22.23 LPA | ₹52 LPA (Zomato) |
| 2023 | ~95% | ₹34.46 LPA | — | ₹60 LPA |

> **2023 was exceptional** due to tech boom. 2024 reflects industry correction. Both are strong by any standard.

**Companies that visited for CSE (2024–25):**
Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Oracle, Zomato, Flipkart, Walmart Labs, Uber, Paytm, Samsung, Infosys, TCS, HCL, IBM, ICICI Bank

## Career Paths After IIT Mandi CSE

| Role | What It Means | Realistic Starting Pay (India) |
|---|---|---|
| Software Development Engineer (SDE) | Build products at tech companies | ₹15–45 LPA |
| ML/AI Engineer | Train and deploy ML models | ₹15–35 LPA |
| Data Engineer | Build data pipelines | ₹12–25 LPA |
| DevOps/Cloud Engineer | Infrastructure, CI/CD | ₹12–22 LPA |
| Quant Analyst | Finance (DE Shaw, Jane Street) | ₹25–60+ LPA |
| Research Scientist | AI labs, DRDO, academia | Varies |
| Startup Founder | Own venture | Varies |

## Higher Education Options
- **M.Tech:** IITs, IISc, IIIT (GATE/PGEE)
- **MS/PhD abroad:** Competitive — Stanford, CMU, UIUC, UT Austin all have strong CS programs
- **MBA:** IIMs via CAT (very popular after 2–3 years work)

## Honest Verdict
**Strongest placement branch at IIT Mandi** — consistently. High demand for software roles means good opportunities even in market downturns. You still need to actively practice DSA (LeetCode), do internships, and build projects. The degree opens doors; walking through them requires effort.

# 3. Mathematics and Computing (MnC)

## What You Study at IIT Mandi

Introduced in 2023. Mathematically the most rigorous B.Tech at IIT Mandi.

**Core Subjects:**
- Real and Complex Analysis
- Abstract Algebra (Groups, Rings, Fields)
- Number Theory
- Combinatorics and Graph Theory
- Numerical Methods and Scientific Computing
- Probability Theory (rigorous)
- Statistical Inference
- Cryptography
- Algorithms and Complexity Theory
- Optimization (convex, combinatorial)
- Mathematical Programming (Python/MATLAB)
- Electives: Computational Finance, Coding Theory, Machine Learning (theory-heavy)

## Career Paths

| Role | Domain | Why MnC Fits |
|---|---|---|
| Quant Analyst / Researcher | Finance (Goldman, DE Shaw, Optiver) | Mathematical depth prized in quant |
| Cryptographer / Security Researcher | Cybersecurity, defense | Number theory + cryptography background |
| ML Researcher | Academic or industry research | Strong theoretical base |
| SDE | Product companies | Coding skills + IIT brand |
| PhD in Math/CS/Stats | Academia | Direct path |
| Actuary | Insurance, finance | Strong probability foundation |

## Higher Education
- MS in Financial Engineering (CMU, NYU Courant, Columbia) — very strong fit
- MS/PhD in Mathematics, Statistics, CS
- MBA

## Honest Verdict
**Placement data is not available yet** (batch hasn't graduated). Based on the field and IIT brand:
- Very strong for quant finance (best branch for this track nationally at good IITs)
- Competitive for software roles — not as "packaged" as CSE but IIT + MnC is respected
- If you love mathematics genuinely, this branch gives the strongest foundation of any B.Tech
- **Do NOT choose for placements alone** — choose if you have real interest in mathematical thinking

# 4. Microelectronics & VLSI

## What You Study at IIT Mandi

Introduced in 2023. India's semiconductor push makes this highly timely.

**Core Subjects:**
- Semiconductor Physics (devices — MOSFETs, BJTs at physics level)
- Analog Circuit Design (CMOS, op-amps)
- Digital Logic and CMOS Design
- VLSI Design Flow (RTL → Synthesis → Place & Route → Tape-out)
- FPGA Design (Verilog/VHDL)
- Digital IC Design
- Analog IC Design
- Physical Design (floor planning, clock tree synthesis)
- Fabrication Technology (process steps — oxidation, lithography, doping)
- Testing and Verification (DFT, timing analysis)
- EDA tools: Cadence, Synopsys, Mentor Graphics

**IIT Mandi Advantage:** **C4DFED (Centre for Design and Fabrication of Electronic Devices)** — IIT Mandi has actual semiconductor fabrication facilities, rare for any IIT.

## Career Paths

| Role | Domain | Companies |
|---|---|---|
| VLSI Physical Design Engineer | Layout, floorplan | Qualcomm, Intel, MediaTek |
| RTL Design / Logic Design | Digital design | AMD, Nvidia, Apple Silicon teams |
| Verification Engineer | SoC verification | Synopsys, ARM, Broadcom |
| Analog IC Design | Amplifiers, PLLs, ADCs | TI, Analog Devices |
| EDA Engineer | Design software tools | Synopsys, Cadence, Mentor |
| Fab Process Engineer | Cleanroom fabrication | Samsung, Micron, Intel Fab |

## Higher Education
- M.Tech VLSI: IIT Madras, IIT Bombay, IISc (IIT Mandi also has M.Tech VLSI)
- MS/PhD: TU Delft, TU Munich, UT Austin, UC San Diego (strong VLSI programs)
- BTech+MTech dual degree at IIT Mandi

## Why This Branch Matters in India Right Now
India's semiconductor mission (₹76,000 crore government incentive package) is actively attracting fabs. Companies like Micron (Gujarat fab), Tata-PSMC (Dholera), and CG Power (Sanand) are hiring. **India needs VLSI engineers more than ever.** Median M.Tech VLSI package at IIT Mandi was ₹21.50 LPA in 2023.

## Honest Verdict
**No B.Tech placement data yet** (2023 batch graduates in 2027). Expected to perform well — VLSI is a high-skill, high-demand niche. Requires comfort with both physics and digital design. Not a software-first branch.

# 7. Engineering Physics (EP)

## What You Study at IIT Mandi

The most fundamental B.Tech — deep physics with engineering application.

**Core Subjects:**
- Classical Mechanics (Lagrangian/Hamiltonian formalism)
- Electrodynamics (Maxwell's equations, wave optics)
- Quantum Mechanics (Schrödinger equation, quantum states)
- Statistical Mechanics and Thermodynamics
- Condensed Matter Physics
- Atomic and Nuclear Physics
- Mathematical Physics (complex analysis, tensors, group theory)
- Computational Physics
- Photonics and Lasers
- Semiconductor Physics
- Engineering electives (from ME, EE, CS departments)

**IIT Mandi Advantage:** Close to Quantum Science research through CQST. Strong faculty in photonics, condensed matter, and quantum technologies.

## Real Placement Data (IIT Mandi)

| Year | Median | Placement Rate |
|---|---|---|
| 2024 | ₹9 LPA | 29.41% |

> **The 29.41% placement rate needs context:** A significant fraction of EP students go for higher studies (IISc, TIFR, PhDs abroad) rather than direct placements. The placement "rate" counts only those who sat for placements. This is not a failure — it reflects the branch's research-oriented character.

**73 students were selected for higher studies** across all IIT Mandi B.Tech branches in 2024 combined. EP contributes a disproportionately high share to this number.

## Career Paths

| Path | Description |
|---|---|
| PhD in Physics/Materials | IISc, TIFR, IITs, or MIT, Princeton, ETH Zurich |
| Quantum Computing Researcher | Emerging — IBM Q, Google Quantum AI, startups |
| Semiconductor R&D | Device physics knowledge is valued |
| Quant Analyst | Finance firms prize physics PhDs |
| Data Scientist | Strong statistical base |
| SDE | Physics grads often strong coders |
| Defense/DRDO researcher | Specialized research positions |

## Higher Education (Primary Path for Most EP Grads)
- **MSc/PhD:** IISc (JEST/GATE-PH), TIFR, JNCASR, IITs
- **PhD abroad:** Strong programs at MIT, Princeton, Caltech, ETH Zurich, Max Planck
- MS in Quantum Computing (very new but growing — IBM, QuTech Netherlands)
- MS in Financial Engineering (extremely strong fit for quant careers)

## Honest Verdict
**Not a branch for immediate high-paying jobs without further study.** This is a research-first degree. If you genuinely love physics and want to work on frontier problems (quantum computing, photonics, materials science), this is the best undergraduate education you can get at IIT Mandi. If you want a well-paying job in 4 years without a PhD, this branch will feel frustrating. Choose it for the right reasons.

# 8. General Engineering

## What It Is — IIT Mandi's Experimental Program

Launched in 2024. India's first IIT program of this kind — genuinely unique. Designed for students who want flexibility in a changing world.

## What You Study

**Years 1–2 (Broad Foundation):**
All students take courses across:
- Mechanical Engineering fundamentals
- Civil Engineering fundamentals
- Data Science and Computer Science
- Mathematics and Physics core
- Engineering design thinking

**Years 3–4 (Choose Your Specialization):**
Students pick from tracks including:
- **Energy Engineering** (renewable energy, storage, smart grids)
- **Advanced Manufacturing** (additive manufacturing, Industry 4.0)
- **E-Mobility / Electric Vehicles** (EV system design, battery tech)
- **Artificial Intelligence and Robotics** (AI applications, robot systems)
- Other specializations (may expand)

**Dual Degree Option:** Program includes an option to pursue integrated MBA alongside B.Tech.

**Industry Co-development:** IIT Mandi is working with industrial partners to co-develop curricula — curriculum stays aligned with current industry requirements.

## Career Paths

| Specialization Track | Careers |
|---|---|
| Energy Engineering | Renewable energy companies, grid operators, government (MNRE) |
| Advanced Manufacturing | Smart factories, Industry 4.0, EV component manufacturing |
| E-Mobility | EV companies (Ather, Ola Electric, Tata EV), automotive OEMs |
| AI and Robotics | AI applications engineering, industrial automation |

## Honest Verdict
**Too new to assess placement outcomes** — 2024 batch graduates in 2028. The concept is good (industry-aligned, flexible). Key uncertainties:
- Alumni network is zero — no seniors to ask
- Employer perception of the "General Engineering" degree vs. a named specialization is unknown
- The flexibility is a feature, but it requires students to be self-directed

**Choose this if:** You genuinely don't know your specific interest yet, or if you're excited by the cross-disciplinary approach and one of the specialization tracks aligns with your goals.
**Don't choose this if:** You want the clearest possible path to a specific job or higher education program.

# 12. BS in Chemical Sciences

A science degree (not engineering B.Tech) — 3 or 4-year BS focused on fundamental chemistry.

**Core Subjects:**
- Organic Chemistry (reactions, mechanisms, synthesis)
- Inorganic Chemistry (coordination compounds, organometallics)
- Physical Chemistry (thermodynamics, kinetics, quantum chemistry)
- Analytical Chemistry (spectroscopy: IR, NMR, Mass Spec)
- Computational Chemistry (molecular modelling)
- Materials Chemistry

# 14. Quantum Science and Engineering

## What It Is

One of IIT Mandi's newest and most forward-looking programs. Connected to **Center for Quantum Science and Technologies (CQST)** established at IIT Mandi in 2023 with a focus on quantum computing, quantum communication, and quantum sensing.

## What You Study (Expected Curriculum)

**Foundation (Years 1–2):**
- Quantum Mechanics (rigorous)
- Linear Algebra and Mathematical Physics
- Classical and Quantum Electrodynamics
- Programming (Python, quantum programming with Qiskit/Cirq)
- Electronics and Circuit Theory

**Core (Years 3–4):**
- Quantum Information Theory
- Quantum Computing (gates, circuits, algorithms)
- Quantum Communication and Cryptography
- Quantum Error Correction
- Quantum Sensing and Metrology
- Photonic Systems (for quantum optics)
- Condensed Matter Physics (quantum materials)
- Quantum Hardware (superconducting qubits, trapped ions, photons)

> **Note:** As of 2026, this is a very new program — curriculum details may evolve. Verify at iitmandi.ac.in.

## Why This Matters — The Global Quantum Race

Global quantum computing market projected to exceed $450 billion by 2030. India has the **National Quantum Mission (NQM)** with ₹6,003 crore funding (2023–2031). IIT Mandi is a recognized node in India's quantum research ecosystem.

**India's quantum priorities:**
- Quantum computing (PARAM Shakti initiative)
- Quantum-secured communication networks
- Quantum-enabled sensing for defense/navigation

## Career Paths

| Role | Timeframe | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Quantum Software Engineer | Near-term | Write quantum algorithms on IBM Q, Google Quantum, IonQ |
| Quantum Hardware Engineer | Medium-term | Cryogenic systems, qubit fabrication |
| Quantum Cryptography Engineer | Near-term | Quantum key distribution, post-quantum cryptography |
| Research Scientist | PhD route | Quantum computing groups at IBM, Google, Microsoft, defense labs |
| Quantum Sensing Engineer | Near-term | Precision magnetometers, gravimeters, LIDAR |
| PhD in Quantum Science | Academic/research | IISc, TIFR, abroad (Caltech, Delft, Waterloo) |

## Honest Verdict
**Very high ceiling, very uncertain near-term floor.** This is genuinely the most cutting-edge B.Tech at IIT Mandi. The quantum computing job market is real but still small globally — most quantum roles today require a PhD. In 4 years (when this batch graduates), the market will be larger. **Ideal profile:** Someone with genuine love for quantum mechanics + physics, comfortable with uncertainty, planning for research or PhD. **Not ideal for:** Someone who wants clear placements in 4 years without PG.

IIT Mandi's CQST is a legitimate research center — you will have real access to quantum research as an undergrad.

# IMPORTANT REALITIES AT IIT MANDI

## Branch Change Policy
Branch change is possible at IIT Mandi after Year 1, based on CGPA. The exact eligibility criteria (minimum CGPA, seat availability) varies — check the official academic rules. It is competitive. Do not choose a branch counting on changing.

## Sector Concentration Risk
**64.2% of IIT Mandi placements were in software/data science in 2024.** This means almost every branch at IIT Mandi has students going into software roles. The IT sector's hiring cycles affect all branches. In good years (2021–23 tech boom), packages were exceptional. In correction years (2024), they moderate. Diversifying skills helps.

## Location Factor
IIT Mandi's Kamand Campus is beautiful but remote. This affects:
- **Internship access:** You will likely need to travel to metro cities for internships. Plan proactively.
- **Company visits:** Fewer companies visit compared to IITs in metro cities — but 192 companies in 2024 is respectable.
- **Network:** The IIT Mandi alumni network is smaller than older IITs — but growing.

## The IIT Brand vs. Branch Dilemma
At IIT Mandi, **choosing a branch you're genuinely interested in** is more important than choosing the "highest-ranked" branch. A CSE student who dislikes programming will underperform a motivated Civil/EP/ME student in the long run. The IIT Mandi brand opens doors — what you do with 4 years determines where you go.

## CGPA Matters
For PhD admissions (domestic and foreign), CGPA is heavily weighted. For software placements, a minimum (usually 6.0–7.0) is required for screening. For PSU via GATE, CGPA cutoffs exist. Maintain at least 7.5+ CGPA in your chosen branch for maximum flexibility.
