# Recruiter Glossary — IIT Mandi

**Product Company:** Builds and sells software/hardware products (e.g., Microsoft, Amazon)
**Service Company:** Provides IT services/consulting (e.g., Infosys, TCS)
**Core Company:** Hires for traditional engineering roles (e.g., L&T, BHEL)
**Analytics Firm:** Focuses on data analysis/consulting (e.g., EXL, Mu Sigma)
**Startup:** Early-stage company, often in tech
**PSU:** Public Sector Undertaking (government company)
**Big 4:** Deloitte, EY, KPMG, PwC (consulting/audit)
