# ME GATE and PSU Pathways at IIT Mandi

**PSU Optionality:**
- ME students can target PSUs like BHEL, NTPC, ONGC, IOCL, SAIL, GAIL, etc.
- GATE ME rank under 1000 (sometimes under 500) is needed for top PSUs.

**Preparation Tips:**
- Start GATE prep by 3rd year; focus on core ME subjects: Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Manufacturing, Strength of Materials, etc.
- Use standard books and join a test series.

**Summary:**
- ME is a good branch for PSU jobs via GATE at IIT Mandi, but competition is intense and private core jobs are fewer.
