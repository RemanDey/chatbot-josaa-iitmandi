# GATE and PSU Opportunities at IIT Mandi

**Overview:**
- GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) is the main exam for M.Tech admissions and PSU (Public Sector Undertaking) jobs in India.
- EE, ME, and CE are the most PSU-aligned branches at IIT Mandi.

**PSU Optionality:**
- EE: High (PowerGrid, NTPC, BHEL, IOCL, etc.)
- ME: Moderate (BHEL, NTPC, ONGC, IOCL, SAIL, GAIL, etc.)
- CE: Moderate (AAI, NHAI, CPWD, ONGC, NHPC, etc.)
- CSE/DSE/EP: Very limited PSU options

**Realistic Competition:**
- GATE is highly competitive; top PSUs require a GATE rank typically under 1000 (sometimes under 200).
- Most IIT Mandi students who succeed in PSU recruitment have dedicated GATE prep from 3rd year onward.

**Branch Advantages:**
- EE: Best for PSU and core government jobs.
- ME/CE: Good for PSU, but private core jobs are fewer.
- CSE: GATE is mainly for M.Tech, not PSU.

**Summary:**
- GATE/PSU is a strong path for EE, ME, and CE at IIT Mandi. Early, focused prep is essential.
