# Placements at IIT Mandi — Fact Sheet

Verified placement statistics and insights for IIT Mandi.

## Overall Statistics (B.Tech, CnP Report)

### 2023-24
- **Placement Rate:** 72.39% of participating B.Tech students were placed.
- **Average CTC:** 22.38 LPA.
- **Median CTC:** 18.5 LPA.

### 2022-23
- **Placement Rate:** 94.09% of participating B.Tech students were placed.
- **Median CTC:** 22 LPA.

## Branch-wise Highlights (2023-24)
- **CSE:** 89.32% placed, Avg 26.55 LPA, Median 22.23 LPA.
- **DSE:** 85.71% placed, Avg 25.92 LPA, Median 22.23 LPA.
- **EE:** 61.66% placed, Avg 17.75 LPA, Median 14 LPA.
- **ME:** 66.67% placed, Avg 13.36 LPA, Median 12 LPA.
- **CE:** 31.25% placed, Avg 10.45 LPA, Median 9 LPA.
- **EP:** 29.41% placed, Avg 9.83 LPA, Median 9 LPA.
- **Bioengineering:** 70% placed, Avg 18.08 LPA, Median 10 LPA.

## Incubation and Startups
- **IIT Mandi Catalyst:** The campus houses a premier startup incubator that provides technical mentoring, networking, office space, and significant seed funding to student and alumni startups.
