# Software Placement Eligibility at IIT Mandi

**Which branches are eligible for software placements?**
- CSE, DSE: Eligible for all software companies.
- EE: Eligible for most, but some top product companies may filter by CGPA or branch.
- ME/EP: Eligible for some, but many companies restrict to circuital branches.
- CE/BioE/BS Chem: Only a few service/IT companies allow all branches; most product/software companies do not.

**Circuital vs Non-Circuital:**
- Circuital: CSE, DSE, EE (sometimes MnC)
- Non-Circuital: ME, CE, EP, BioE, Chem, Agri, Quantum, IMBA

**OA/Interview Filtering:**
- Many companies filter by branch at the OA (online assessment) or interview stage.
- Some allow all branches for OA, but shortlist only circuital branches for interviews.

**Summary:**
- CSE/DSE have the broadest software placement eligibility. EE is next. ME/EP/CE/BioE have limited eligibility; check each year’s placement brochure for details.
