# Research vs Placements at IIT Mandi

**Overview:**
- Students at IIT Mandi often choose between research/higher studies and campus placements.

**Research Path:**
- Focus on CGPA, research projects, and faculty recommendations
- Pursue internships (SURGE, MITACS, DAAD, etc.)
- Apply for MS/PhD in India or abroad

**Placement Path:**
- Focus on DSA, coding, and internships for software roles
- Build a strong resume and network with recruiters

**Branch Trends:**
- EP, EE, and some ME/CE students often pursue higher studies
- CSE/DSE students more likely to take placements

**Summary:**
- Both paths are viable; choose based on your interests and strengths.
