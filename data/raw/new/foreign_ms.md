# Foreign MS after IIT Mandi B.Tech

**Overview:**
- Many IIT Mandi students pursue MS/PhD abroad, especially in the USA, Europe, and Canada.

**Key Steps:**
- Maintain a strong CGPA (usually 8.0+)
- Build research experience (projects, publications, internships)
- Secure strong letters of recommendation
- Prepare for GRE/TOEFL/IELTS as required
- Write a compelling Statement of Purpose (SOP)

**Popular Destinations:**
- USA (Stanford, MIT, UIUC, UT Austin, etc.)
- Europe (ETH Zurich, TU Munich, EPFL, etc.)
- Canada (Toronto, UBC, Waterloo, etc.)
- Singapore (NUS, NTU)

**Summary:**
- Foreign MS/PhD is a strong path for IIT Mandi students, especially those interested in research and academia.
