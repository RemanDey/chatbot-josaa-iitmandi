## How to Reach IIT Mandi

### Campus Location
- **Main Campus:** Kamand, Himachal Pradesh — 175005
- **North Campus:** Approximately 23 km from Mandi bus stand (40–45 min drive)
- **Contact:** +91-1905-267015 | registrar@iitmandi.ac.in

### By Road
- Delhi → Mandi: 402 km (~8 hours)
- Chandigarh → Mandi: 164 km (~3.5 hours)
- Ropar → Mandi: 120 km (~2.5 hours)
- Public buses: HP Roadways, Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana roadways. Most operate at night and reach Mandi early morning.
- Private luxury buses available from Delhi and Chandigarh.

### Mandi to Campus (Local)
- Himachal roadways and private buses ply from Mandi bus terminal to Kamand/Salgi village during day time.
- Cabs available 24x7 outside Mandi bus stand.
- **Cab fare: Rs. 800–1000 from Mandi bus stand to campus.**
- Institute transport: available for guests/visitors on request (subject to availability).

### By Rail
Nearest railway stations:
- Kiratpur
- Ropar (Rupnagar)
- Chandigarh

### By Air
- **Bhuntar Airport (Kullu):** 42 km (~1.5 hours by road) — nearest, 1–2 domestic flights from Delhi/Chandigarh
- **Gaggal Airport (Kangra/Dharamshala):** 175 km (~4.5 hours)
- **Chandigarh Airport:** 169 km (~3.5 hours)

## Campus Facilities

- **Health Centre:** On campus
- **Sports Facilities:** Basketball, badminton, cricket, lawn tennis, and others; Inter-IIT participation
- **Student Gymkhana:** Student body managing clubs and activities
- **LMS (Moodle):** https://lms.iitmandi.ac.in
- **Guidance & Counseling Service (GCS):** https://students.iitmandi.ac.in/main/gcs/
- **Anti-Ragging:** IIT Mandi has zero-tolerance anti-ragging policy; no significant ragging incidents reported
- **No mall or cinema in Mandi city** — nearest cinema hall is in Chandigarh (~4 hours)
- **Weekend travel:** Manali (~3 hours), Prashar Lake (popular weekend spot), Shimla (~4 hours)
