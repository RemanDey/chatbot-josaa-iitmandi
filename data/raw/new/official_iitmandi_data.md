## Fee Structure (2025-26)

- BTech/BS/MBA/MSc/MA/I-PhD students — Hostel charge: Rs. 9,600 per semester (vacation category)
- BTech/BS/MBA/MSc/MA/I-PhD students — Mess charge: approximately Rs. 15,360 per semester
- These are charges for existing students (Even Semester 2025-26)

## Mess Menu

Common breakfast items at IIT Mandi mess include: paratha, poori chana, idli, uttapam, dosa, milk, bread, butter and jam, fruits, sprouts, tea, coffee, and Bournvita.

Lunch and dinner include vegetarian dishes such as rajma, kadhi pakora, paneer dishes, dal, rice, roti, sabzi, and mixed vegetables.

Non-vegetarian options are served on specific days.

## Branches and Programmes at IIT Mandi

### BTech General Engineering
- Offered by: School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering (SMME)
- Specializations available: AI & Robotics, Mechatronics & AI, Communications Technology, Open Specialisation
- Students spend first year in general courses across all branches, then choose specialization from second year onwards
- Fintech specialization also available

### BS Chemical Sciences
- Total credit requirement: 163 credits
- First semester includes IC 152 — Introduction to Python and Data Science

### Other BTech Branches
- Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
- Electrical Engineering (EE) — covers ECE as well, no separate ECE branch
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- Mathematics and Computing (MnC)
- Microelectronics and VLSI
- Bio-Engineering
- Engineering Physics (EP)
- Civil Engineering
- Materials Science and Engineering (MSE)
