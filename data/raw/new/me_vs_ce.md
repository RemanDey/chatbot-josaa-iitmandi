# ME vs CE at IIT Mandi

**ME (Mechanical Engineering):**
- Focus on mechanics, design, manufacturing
- Best for GATE/PSU and core engineering
- Software transition is possible but hard

**CE (Civil Engineering):**
- Focus on structures, geotech, environment
- Best for GATE/PSU and government jobs
- Software transition is very rare

**Summary:**
- ME is more versatile; CE is more specialized. Both are core-heavy and best for those interested in field/lab work.
