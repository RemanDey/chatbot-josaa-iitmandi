# IIT Mandi DSE — Placement Details

## Source and Scope

- Source: IIT Mandi CnP Cell report.
- Metrics below are for participating students in each year.

## Verified Placement Metrics (CnP Report)

| Year | Placed % | Avg CTC | Median CTC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2023-24 | 85.71% | 25.92 LPA | 22.23 LPA |
| 2022-23 | 97% | 32.3 LPA | 32.22 LPA |

## Expected Role Categories

- Data Analyst / Data Scientist: primary destination
- ML Engineer / AI Engineer: growing demand
- SWE (software engineering general): possible with DSA prep
- Business Analytics: service sector
- Research roles: some students

## Key Expected Recruiters

- Companies with data science teams: Amazon, Microsoft, Flipkart (data teams)
- Analytics companies: EXL Analytics, WNS, Mu Sigma
- AI/ML startups: various
- Same recruiters as CSE but for data-specific roles

## Critical Note for Counseling

- DSE is too new to have a proven placement track record
- Students should treat DSE placement expectations as "CSE-adjacent but less proven"
- Students who are specifically data-science focused: DSE is appropriate
- Students wanting more certainty about placement outcomes: CSE at IIT Mandi or other established programs may be safer

## PPO and Internship

- Pattern expected to mirror CSE; actual data insufficient
- Data science internships exist at mid-large companies
- Research internships in ML/AI: natural fit for DSE students
