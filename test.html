IIT Mandi Undergraduate Seat Matrix Overview

IIT Mandi offers an approximate total pool of 630 seats across its standard 4-year undergraduate tracks (B.Tech and BS programs) through JoSAA counseling.

Note on Seat Pools: The total intake includes both the Gender-Neutral pool and the Female Supernumerary pool (which adds extra seats to achieve the required 20% female enrollment mandated by the ministry).

Category-Wise Seat Allocation Quota

  • Open Category (General): ~40.5%
  • OBC-NCL: 27%
  • Scheduled Caste (SC): 15%
  • General-EWS: 10%
  • Scheduled Tribe (ST): 7.5%

Additional Management Admissions

Alongside the technical courses, the School of Management intakes 80 candidates for its programs. Please note that admissions for this track are managed independently by the institute and are not processed through JoSAA counseling.
