1. Verified Institute-Specific Facts

\n

FACT: IIT Mandi has an active student club culture across technical, cultural, sports, and social domains.

\n

FACT: All clubs operate under the Students' Gymkhana, the apex student body at IIT Mandi.

\n

FACT: Each club is headed by a President and Secretary, elected or selected each academic year.

\n

FACT: Faculty advisors are assigned to each club for institutional oversight.

\n

FACT: Club activities are funded partly through the Students' Activity Fund collected each semester.

\n

FACT: Most clubs hold open recruitment drives in the first 2–4 weeks of the odd semester (August–September).

\n

FACT: Recruitment may involve written tests, portfolio submission, auditions (for cultural clubs), or informal interviews.

\n

FACT: Students can join multiple clubs simultaneously; there is no formal restriction.

\n

FACT: WhatsApp groups and notice board announcements are the primary channels for recruitment calls.

\n

FACT: Clubs are allocated space in designated club rooms on the Kamand campus.

\n

FACT: Equipment and resources are managed by club coordinators and stored in these rooms.

\n

FACT: Utsav is the annual cultural fest organized by the cultural clubs.

\n

FACT: Exodia is the annual technical fest organized by the technical clubs.

\n

FACT: Inter-hostel competitions and sports meets are organized by the Sports Council.

\n

FACT: Smaller club-level workshops, talks, and competitions happen year-round.

\n

FACT: Clubs submit annual budget proposals to the Students' Gymkhana.

\n

FACT: Sponsorships from companies are pursued independently by clubs, especially for fests.

\n

FACT: Students are not required to pay separate club membership fees.

\n

FACT: Types of clubs available include Technical (Robotics, Coding/Programming, Electronics, Design), Cultural (Music, Dance, Drama/Theatre, Photography, Fine Arts, Literary), Sports (Cricket, Football, Basketball, Badminton, Athletics, Chess), Social/Outreach (NSS, Environment Club), and Entrepreneurship (E-Cell).

\n

FACT: IIT Mandi has a Sports Council that governs all sports activities including inter-hostel tournaments, inter-IIT contingents, and access to sports facilities on the Kamand campus.

\n

FACT: The Sports Council is headed by a Sports Secretary (student) and supported by a Sports Officer from the administration.

\n

FACT: Individual sports have designated student coordinators or captains.

\n

FACT: Available outdoor sports facilities include courts for Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, and Badminton.

\n

FACT: Available indoor sports include Table Tennis, Chess, Carrom, and an indoor Badminton court.

\n

FACT: A gymnasium with basic equipment is available on campus.

\n

FACT: A swimming pool is available, but current availability needs verification with the Sports Officer.

\n

FACT: Inter-hostel competitions are held across multiple sports each semester and are open to all students.

\n

FACT: Points tables are maintained for inter-hostel competitions; the hostel with the most wins receives a trophy or recognition at the end of the year.
